Preparation Strategy

• Divide prep into Foundation, Consolidation, and Revision phases.
• Even 3 focused hours daily outweigh 10 distracted hours.
• Prioritize Bailey as your base; add Schwartz or Sabiston selectively.
• Solve MCQs daily (50–150/day) and maintain a mistake journal.
• Attempt mock exams every 5–7 days; analyze errors immediately.
• Reserve your last 15–20 days for revision and active recall.
• Follow the 1–3–7–15 day spaced recall cycle.
• Target 45 seconds per MCQ for exam-time efficiency.
